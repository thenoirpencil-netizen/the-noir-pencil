Polish

Proofreading

The final pass. For writing that is already well-developed and needs a careful, thorough review before it goes anywhere.

We check grammar, spelling, punctuation, capitalization, and formatting consistency throughout your document. Nothing is rewritten. Nothing is restructured. What you have stays intact. We just make sure nothing is standing between your writing and your reader.

Best for writing that is complete and ready for a professional final review.

Refine

Line and Copy Editing

Most Popular

For writing that has something to say and deserves to say it as clearly as possible.

Refine includes everything in Polish, then goes further. We work at the sentence level: tightening what drags, smoothing what stumbles, sharpening word choice, and bringing tone into consistency across the full document. Your voice stays at the center of every decision. We are refining how it comes through, not changing what it is.

This is the service most of our clients need.

Transform

Developmental Editing

For writing that needs the bigger picture worked out before the language is refined.

Transform is our most in-depth editorial service. We look at the document as a whole: how it is organized, how the argument or narrative builds, where the reader gets lost, what is missing, and what needs to be reconsidered. This is strategic, structural work. The ideas stay yours. The direction stays yours. We help you see the architecture of your writing so you can make it work the way you intended.

Because of the depth of this service, every Transform project begins with an intake conversation before booking is confirmed.

Starting at $2,000. Custom quote only.

To begin, submit an inquiry and we will schedule a brief conversation before moving forward.
